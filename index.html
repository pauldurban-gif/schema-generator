What is Schema Markup?

Schema markup (also called structured data) is code you add to your website that helps search engines and AI models like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity understand your organization's key information. Think of it as a "nutrition label" for your website‚Äîit clearly labels who you are, what you do, where you're located, and how people can contact you.

Why is it Important?

When AI models search the web to answer user questions, they prioritize websites with clear, structured data. Schema markup helps your organization appear in AI-generated answers with:

  • Contact Information: Phone and email in search results
  • Location & Hours: Where to find you and when you're available
  • Services & Programs: Clear descriptions of what you offer
  • Team Members: Names and roles that build trust
  • Social Media: Easy links for engagement

Bottom line: Schema markup makes it easier for people to decide to contact, visit, or support your organization.
